Project Lifecycle — How I Run a Ticket

From TeamDynamix intake to delivery, automation, and ongoing improvements. Below is the full workflow I follow.

1) Intake

Review new TeamDynamix ticket. Understand objectives and acknowledge receipt.

2) Triage & Scope

Determine urgency, clarity, requester fit; consult with boss for true scope and time balancing.

3) Data & Access

Identify sources, existing APIs, or data flow; normalize and document pipeline for new data.

4) Build & Analyze

Choose platform (Qlik, Python, etc.), analyze and visualize; iterate if needed.

5) Deliver & Review

Share dashboard/report, collect feedback, revise as needed. Notify stakeholder.

6) Automate & Schedule

Set up refresh schedules, logging, and alerts using tools like VisualCron.

7) Govern & Secure

Assign access based on sensitivity, section rules, and university compliance.

8) Maintain & Improve

Track usage, adapt to evolving needs, update for new semesters or requests.
